Who We Are

Our Vision & Mission

Driven by compassion, guided by integrity — everything we do is in service of the animals in our care.

Our Vision

A future where all animals are safe, respected, and loved in homes of their own.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing sanctuary for animals awaiting their forever homes, ensuring their health and wellbeing through dedicated care, daily enrichment, and medical support.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Four principles that guide every decision we make and every animal we care for.

Compassion

Every animal is treated with the love and dignity they deserve.

Integrity

We are honest, transparent, and accountable in every action we take.

Dedication

We are unwavering in our commitment to the animals in our care.

Community

We believe that by working together, we can change lives.

Our Philosophy

We Believe...

These beliefs shape the way we care for every animal and engage with our community.

Every animal's life is worth the effort. Every dog and cat, regardless of their past, deserves a future full of love and care.

Sanctuary means more than a shelter. Health and wellbeing require active love, enrichment, and play — not just a roof and a meal.

Every animal has a perfect match. Through patience and careful vetting, we can find the right home for every individual in our care.

Collaboration creates change. By engaging with foster carers, volunteers, and our community, we can end animal vulnerability.

Responsible care is the foundation. Regular veterinary support, vaccinations, and desexing are essential steps toward a healthier animal community.

About Us

Who We Are

Cape Animal Protection Shelter Inc. (CAPS) is a registered nonprofit organisation based in Weipa, Cape York, Queensland. We are run almost entirely by volunteers, united by a single purpose: giving every animal the chance they deserve.

Our small team of casual caretakers works rotating shifts to ensure our animals receive consistent, loving care. Our volunteer Board provides the governance, strategy, and organisational backbone that keeps CAPS running.

Animals in our care are listed through SaviourLife, connecting them with prospective adopters across Australia.
